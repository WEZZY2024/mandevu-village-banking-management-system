What is village Banking ?

" Village banking is a way for people who know and trust each other(like friends,coworkers , or neighbors) to save and borrow money together , without needing a bank. A typical group has 5 to 10 members.They set their own rules, elect leaders , and meet once in a week normally a friday to give out loans , track repayments and track there savings.Everyone contributes , and members can apply for loans from the group's shared savings. At the end of the cycle (usually 12 months) members get back their savings plus a share of the interest earned .The more you save and credit the more you earn."

" Traditionally , all of this is tracked using notebooks , cash boxes , or spreadsheets - but that comes with risk and confusion .Chimunzi digitizes it all: saving , lending , record-keeping, and payouts, securely from your phone in the comfort of your home."
